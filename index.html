I'm Wendy Liu, and I live in London.

I write and speak about technology from a socialist perspective.

Before I discovered the "left", I was a software engineer and startup founder (see "Silicon Inquiry").

This website is meant as a record of things I've done. To get in touch, find me on Twitter or email me.

writing

Most of my writing is for New Socialist (I'm an economics editor) and Notes From Below (I'm the web editor). I'm also writing a book for Repeater: Abolish Silicon Valley (2020).

I recently finished a masters degree on inequality at the LSE and am sharing my notes/essays/dissertation.

For a list of nearly everything I've ever published, see Writing.

speaking

I occasionally do talks/panels/podcasts on topics at the intersection of tech and the left.

For a chronological list of my past & upcoming speaking appearances (podcasts, talks, panels, etc), see Speaking.
